Characters in Noli Me Tanghere
Pilosopo Tasio Pilosopo Tasio is an old scholar who lives in San Diego. Although he is smart some think he's out of his mind because of his crazy ideas and his perceptions on Christianity.
Pedro Pedro is the husband of Sisa. He's also the father of Basilio and Crispin. He is a representation of how abusive the relationships of families were back then.
Sisa Sisa is Pedro's wife. Despite being abused she still cared for her husband because she didn't have anywhere else to go. When her Children went missing she went crazy and started searching for them endlessly.
Crispin The son of both Sisa and Pedro. He was always against abuse and the way his father treated his family and he always planned on running away from his father along with his mother and brother. When he got older, he ran away to work for Chrisostomo Ibarra.
Basilio Basilio is the brother of Crispin. He served as a church boy in the church at San Diego. He later became one of Ibbara's Allies
